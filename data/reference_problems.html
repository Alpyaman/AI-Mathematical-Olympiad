Problem 3

ID: 424e18

The winner of each race in the $i^{\text{th}}$ round receives $2^{20-i}$ points and the loser gets no points. At the end of the tournament, we rank the competitors according to their scores. Let $N$ denote the number of possible orderings of the competitors at the end of the tournament. Let $k$ be the largest positive integer such that $10^k$ divides $N$. What is the remainder when $k$ is divided by $10^{5}$?

Answer: 160
